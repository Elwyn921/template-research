# Research Integrity Contract

Reportable outputs must be traceable to source evidence, dataset versions,
configuration, code, generated artifacts, and review notes.

Foundry assets must not invent:

- data;
- citations;
- mechanisms;
- financial facts;
- model metrics;
- experimental results;
- causal conclusions;
- investment recommendations.

Downstream projects must preserve source IDs, dataset IDs, experiment/run IDs,
figure IDs, claim IDs, and release IDs when using Foundry-provided assets.

Automated tools may draft, structure, validate, and audit. They do not replace
project-level judgment or human review for scientific, financial, causal, or
release decisions.
