# Industry Evidence Contract

Industry, company, policy, commercial-space, and investment research must
preserve:

- event date;
- disclosure date;
- as-of date;
- source tier;
- confidence;
- entity linkage;
- region or sector scope when relevant;
- assumptions and caveats.

The workflow must explicitly protect against:

- look-ahead bias;
- unverified company claims;
- marketing-source overreliance;
- unsupported valuation assumptions.

Investment and strategy conclusions require human review.
