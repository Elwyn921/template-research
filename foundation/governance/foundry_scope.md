# Foundry Scope

Research Foundry maintains reusable ResearchOS capabilities:

- contracts;
- schemas;
- validators;
- templates;
- tool registries;
- adapters;
- capability packs;
- profiles;
- bootstrap and export tooling.

The Foundry does not maintain project-specific research questions, evidence,
data, experiments, claims, figures, manuscripts, investment theses, or release
decisions.

Downstream projects receive versioned assets through controlled export. They do
not dynamically inherit Foundry governance after export.
